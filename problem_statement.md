# Problem Statement

Small and mid-size manufacturing businesses generate a constant stream of unstructured notes:
— client emails, WhatsApp messages, factory floor updates
— that someone has to manually read, interpret, and act on. 
This is slow, easy to get wrong under time pressure, and creates no record of why a decision was made.

This project explores whether an AI agent can take over the first pass of that work:
— reading the note, drafting what should happen next 
— while keeping a human firmly in control of anything that actually goes out or gets acted on.
The goal isn't to remove the person from the loop; it's to remove the slow, repetitive part and leave the judgment call with them, with a clear record of every decision.

## Who this is for
Small business owners or operations staff who get too many notes to process manually but can't afford to send anything without reviewing it first.

## What "success" looks like
- The agent correctly drafts a response/action from a note most of the time
- It never sends or finalizes anything without explicit human approval
- Every action is logged, so any decision can be traced back later
